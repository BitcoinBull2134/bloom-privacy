# Bloom — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 2026

Bloom is a wellness companion for pregnancy and gestational diabetes,
for educational and informational purposes only.

## What we collect: nothing.

- All data you enter — glucose readings, meals, weight, appointments,
  photos, journal letters, and settings — is stored **only on your device**.
- Bloom has no user accounts, no servers, and no analytics.
- We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.
- Deleting the app deletes all of its data.

## Sharing you control

When you export a PDF report or copy a recipe, that content is shared
only through the apps **you** choose in your phone's share sheet.

## Not medical advice

Bloom does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
Always follow your healthcare provider's guidance.
